Sabato Marco Siniscalchi — Generative Error Correction for Automatic Speech Recognition

Generative error correction is a novel approach to improve the performance of automatic speech recognition (ASR) systems. This talk will cover the combination of Natural Language and Speech Processing to develop a generative error correction model for ASR. Full details of the talk will be available soon.
