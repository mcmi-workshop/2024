MCMI 2024 - Important Dates

  • Workshop Paper Submission Deadline: 1 July 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: 1 September 2024
  • Camera Ready Version and Registration: 27 September 2024
  • Workshop Date: 1 December 2024

All deadlines refer to midnight (23:59) in the AoE (Anywhere on Earth) time zone. For the camera-ready version, at least one author of each accepted paper must register for the workshop. Papers that are not presented at the workshop will not be included in the proceedings.

All accepted papers will be published in Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS), Springer
